About AIISC
Where AI Meets Industry

The AI Institute of South Carolina brings together researchers, engineers, and industry partners to solve the problems that matter. We bridge the gap between what AI can do in a lab and what it needs to do in the real world.

Our work spans natural language processing, knowledge graphs, computer vision, health informatics, and neuro-symbolic AI — always with an eye toward practical deployment.

Natural Language Processing Knowledge Graphs Neuro-Symbolic AI Explainable AI Health Informatics Computer Vision
